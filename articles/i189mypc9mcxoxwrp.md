# who is narendra modi?
- **Name**: Narendra Damodardas Modi
- **Birth**: September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat
- **Roles**: Prime Minister of India since 2014, former Chief Minister of Gujarat (2001-2014)
- **Affiliations**: Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS)
- **Education**: Master's degree in Political Science from Gujarat University
- **Background**: Born into a poor family, exposed to the RSS at age eight, became a full-time RSS worker in 1971, and entered BJP in 1985.
# 1. What are Narendra Modi's major achievements as Prime Minister
**Narendra Modi's Major Achievements as Prime Minister:**

1. **Economic Growth**: India's GDP grew at an average rate of about 5% during his initial eight years in office, with efforts to liberalize foreign direct investment[5].

2. **Make in India**: Launched in 2014 to transform India into a global manufacturing hub[2][4].

3. **StartUp India**: Encouraged entrepreneurship by providing a favorable business environment[4].

4. **Swachh Bharat Abhiyan**: Over 12 crore toilets built across the country[1].

5. **Ayushman Bharat**: The world's largest healthcare program, covering over 50 crore Indians[2].

6. **Aadhaar Rollout**: Implemented a digital ID system to streamline welfare delivery[3].

7. **Electricity Access**: Increased electricity access in villages from 88% to 99.6%[3].

8. **Financial Inclusion**: Increased financial institution account ownership from 48.3% to 71.1%[3].

9. **Poverty Reduction**: Almost 25 crore people escaped multidimensional poverty[2].

10. **Gender Inclusiveness**: Efforts to promote gender equality and women's empowerment[4].

11. **Annexation of Kashmir**: Abolished Article 370, integrating Kashmir more closely with the Indian union[1].
